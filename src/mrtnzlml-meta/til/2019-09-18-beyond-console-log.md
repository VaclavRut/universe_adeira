There is more than just a simple `console.log`: https://medium.com/@mattburgess/beyond-console-log-2400fdf4a9d8

- `console.log/warn/error/info` with `%s`, `%o` and `%c`
- `console.dir`
- `console.table`
- `console.assert`
- `console.count/countReset`
- `console.trace`
- `console.time/timeEnd`
- `console.group/groupCollapsed/groupEnd`
